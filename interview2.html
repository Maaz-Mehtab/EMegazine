  • Question 6. How can student participate in Microsoft Imagine cup?
  • Answer: It is a simple process to participate in Microsoft Imagine cup. In Microsoft Imagine cup there are 3 different categories for participation: Innovation, Game Development and World-citizenship. If you have you have an innovative idea on which you are working or if you are making the next big game or you have an idea that can solve the problem around your society you can participate in the relevant category. The process of applying and participating is easy. 1. Create an account on www.imaginecup.com and apply in the category of desire. 2. Create a team and build you Minimum viable product (MVP). 3. Keep updating and grooming your product until the end.
